Core story

Type 2 is a progressive condition driven by insulin resistance and high blood sugar. The usual goal is to bring A1C and fasting glucose into a safer range and protect heart, kidneys, nerves, and eyes.

Typical plan

  • Gradual weight loss if needed
  • Moderate-carb, lower-sugar eating pattern
  • Regular movement & basic strength work
  • Medication as needed (e.g. metformin, GLP-1s)
